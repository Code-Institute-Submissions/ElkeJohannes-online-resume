My specialities where in the Windows deployments, with a focus on Server 2008R2 and Hyper-V. Additionally I implemented a lot of clustered Exchange environments.

Note: pinch of salt required while reading text below.
A very important, but often overlooked aspect of being a consultant is the ability to sense when a customer requires more coffee. So for example I was at one site once where this systemsadministrator was like: 'I don't drink coffee'. But using my fifth sense as a professional consultant, I knew that what he meant was he needed coffee now more than ever but simply had never had good coffee. So I went and got him some, then locked him up in his office to enjoy it in privacy. That was my last day on the job there, but I'm sure someone found him and let him out. It's fine. Probably.
